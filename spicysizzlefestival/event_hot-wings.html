Hot Ones Hot Wings Challenge

Hot Ones presents:
A heat-packed challenge with the hottest wings

Get ready to feel the heat with this spicy challenge brought to you by Hot Ones, the hit YouTube show where celebrities answer questions as they attempt to finish a round of spicy hot chicken wings.

Now you can see if you have what it takes to handle hot wings coated in the spiciest hot sauces! Starting from a Scoville rating of 2200, each hot sauce is increasingly spicier than the one before. With ten chicken wings and ten even spicier hot sauces, can you last until the final round against Hot One's own The Last Dab hot sauce?

The Last Dab is the final sauce in the hot wings challenge. At a Scoville rating of 2,000,000+, you'll definitely be feeling the heat. But don't worry, there'll be lots of milk and water provided so you don't completely burn up...

Winners will be awarded with exclusive Hot Ones merchandise and a bottle of The Last Dab hot sauce to take home.

The Hot Ones Hot Wings challenge starts at 12:00 at the events area. Be warned, this challenge is not for the faint hearted!
