Spicy Sizzle Festival

Get ready for the Spicy Sizzle this coming January 15th!

A brand new food festival experience coming to Silo Park this summer.

At the festival will be a wide variety of spicy food from different cuisines from all around the world. There'll be lots of food stalls and food trucks at Silo Park serving the spiciest Indian curry, Vietnamese pho, American buffalo wings and Chinese dumplings, just to name a few!

There'll also be some fun activities at the festival such as spicy food challenges to see if you can handle the spiciest dishes. Learn how to cook spicy dishes with professional chefs at the cooking classes or take home your own custom-made hot sauce.

Come experience the heat!

Information Desk

The information desk is located at the entrance of the festival at the corner of Madden Street and Beaumont Street. There are first aid and cash out facilities at the desk.

Public Transport

For more specific information on public transport in Auckland, visit the Auckland Transport website.

By Bus

From the North Shore, disembark on Fanshawe Street at Victoria Park. Then walk towards Silo Park along Beaumont Street or Daldy Street.

From the rest of Auckland, disembark at the closest stop to the Britomart Transport Centre. Then walk along Quay Street, over the Wynyard Crossing and along the North Wharf Promenade to Silo Park.

By Train

Disembark at the Britomart Transport Centre. Then walk along Quay Street, over the Wynyard Crossing and along the North Wharf Promenade to Silo Park.

By Ferry

Disembark at the Ferry Building and walk along Quay Street, over the Wynyard Crossing and along the North Wharf Promenade to Silo Park.

Parking

The closest public carpark is the Wynyard Carpark on Pakenham Street. From there, walk along Pakenham Street then Daldy Street to get to Silo Park. Alternatively, there is the AT Downtown Carpark on Customs Street. From there, walk along Quay Street, over the Wynyard Crossing and along the North Wharf Promenade to Silo Park.
