Info

When? Times and Dates

Aquanaut will run through the duration of the Auckland Arts Festival from March 10th to 27th. Opening times are 9:00am to 9:00pm.

Where? Location

Aquanaut is situated in the Hunua Rooms in the Aotea Centre.

50 Mayoral Drive, Auckland CBD, Auckland 1010

Getting Here

For more specific information on public transport in Auckland, visit the Auckland Transport website.

The nearest public carpark is the AT Civic Car Park which is directly connected to the Aotea Centre. Entry to the carpark is located on Greys Ave off Mayoral Drive. Take the lift to Level 1 of the Aotea Centre. For more details on the AT Civic Car Park visit the Auckland Transport website.

Map

Access

The Hunua Rooms are located on Level 1 of the Aotea Centre. There will be signage and floor markings guiding your way from the main entrance and carpark.

School Visits

Aquanaut is more than happy to accommodate for school visits. We're here to help you plan and book your school visit. Booking is essential and we ask you to book atleast one week in advance.

To organise a school visit or for additional information, contact us.
